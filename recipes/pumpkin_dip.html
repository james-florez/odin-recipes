Pumpkin Dip Recipe

This addictive pumpkin dip was first shown to me by my 3rd grade teacher and my family has made it for Thanksgiving every year since.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz Cream Cheese
  • 2/3 cup Brown Sugar
  • 2/3 cup Canned Pumpkin
  • 3 teaspoons Maple Syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon
  • Apple Slices/li>
  • Pretzels

Steps

  1. Place first 3 ingredients in a bowl and beat with a mixer at medium speed until well blended.
  2. Add syrup and cinnamon and beat until smooth.
  3. Cover and chill for 30 minutes.
  4. Serve with apple slices and pretzels

Chicken Curry Wraps

These simple and unique chicken curry wraps are perfect for an easy lunch while hiking and backpacking. I was introduced to this recipe by my friend Jack Vorster while we were working as backpacking guides for UCLA Outdoor Adventures.

Ingredients

  • 1 Foil Packet of Chicken
  • 1 2.5 oz Ranch Dressing Packet
  • 1/8 cup Raisins
  • 1/8 cup cashews
  • 1/2 tablespoon Curry Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon Cayenne
  • 1/8 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon Pepper
  • 2 Tortillas or Pitas

Steps

  1. Before leaving for your trip add the spices, cashews, and raisins to a gallon ziploc bag.
  2. When you are ready to eat add the chicken and ranch and mix everything together by massaging the bag.
  3. Scoop the result on to a tortilla or pita.
  4. Enjoy!
